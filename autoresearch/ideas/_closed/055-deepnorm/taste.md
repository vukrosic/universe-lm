# Taste log — 055 DeepNorm

## r1 — 2026-06-11 — verdict: reject
- **Same-paper confirmatory re-run, not a new bet.** 017-sub-ln-sandwich (DeepNet §3.1, same Wang et al. paper, arXiv:2203.00555) already ran NULL at 12L; closed.md:34 closure note reads "expected null at 6L, lever fires at 100+ layers per DeepNet". DeepNorm (§3.2 of the same paper) carries the same depth-stability bet — the paper's headline result is 200L vs 48L, with the divergence/convergence gap over Pre-LN appearing past ~50 layers. We are at 12.
- **The idea card concedes the null.** "If DeepNorm helps at tiny1m3m, then unstable update geometry; if it does not, the benefit is probably only realized in much deeper stacks" — that *is* the already-logged conclusion from 017. Re-running it doesn't teach us anything 017 didn't.
- **Niche mismatch.** At N=12, α=(2·12)^(1/4)≈2.21 and β=(8·12)^(-1/4)≈0.32 — non-zero scaling, but at the depth tier where Pre-LN already trains cleanly without it; the paper's bf16-stability claim is fundamentally about training at 100s of layers (compute we don't have). transfer-risk on the card is marked `low` for "deep transformer training" — true, but only at depth tiers out of scope for this pipeline.
- **Portfolio crowding.** Active norm/init queue already includes 017 (done null), 029 v-norm (running), 030 unet-skip-sigmoid (running), 051 scalenorm, 052 fixnorm, and 056-branchnorm — 056 is DeepNorm's direct sibling from the same authors' depth-branch-rescaling axis. Adding 055 increases the norm-zoo backlog without information value.
- **How to make this slot earn its keep:** if the miner wants to keep a DeepNet-family lever live, frame it as a *composition* with a known WIN (e.g., DeepNorm-α scaling on top of 023 Canon or 009 FIRE) where the bet is "depth-bounded residual + existing attention lever = additive" — that's a new bet, not a re-run of 017. As filed, 055 is a re-litigation.
