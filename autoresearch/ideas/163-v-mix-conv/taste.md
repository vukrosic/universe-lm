# 163 — Post-Attention V-Mix Depthwise Convolution (taste)

## r1 — 2026-06-14 — verdict: accept
- **Axis-completion bet, not a vibes bet.** This is the third axis of a deliberate 3-axis locality test the miner designed: 143-shortconv (pre-attn depthwise conv, borderline-WIN-rule at tiny1m3m), 157-conv-ffn (post-FFN-activation depthwise conv, null at Δ=-0.0078), 163-v-mix-conv (post-attention, on V before W_O). A WIN localizes the gain to "post-softmax V-axis"; a NULL closes the post-attention locality axis at 0.94M alongside the closed pre-attention and post-FFN axes. Both outcomes are informative — passes the clean-null-result bar.
- **Sharp sentence of "we expect X because Y".** Init `[0,1,0]` per channel ⇒ step-0 strict identity (bit-identical to baseline, not just close). With k=3 post-softmax V-mix, depthwise, +2,304 params (+0.25% of 0.94M), 12 LoC. If post-attention locality is binding, expect Δ<0; if it isn't, expect Δ inside ±0.04 noise band (matching the 143/157 cluster's null-band behavior).
- **Niche fit is clean.** Mechanism (not HP). Identity-init-able. Step-0 byte-identical. +0.25% param budget is below the 1% "free" threshold. Plausibly shows effect at tiny1m3m (matches 143/157's regime; both produced measureable signals at this scale).
- **Transfer risk is correctly tagged `low`.** Source is Hyena (Poli et al. 2023, arXiv:2302.10866), validated at Striped Hyena 7B; conv-on-V is a published / reproducible architecture at ≥100M source scale. The non-depthwise Hyena hierarchy is the tier-mismatch lever; the *residual* post-attn V-conv form (163) is distinct from full Hyena replacement and is what Striped Hyena actually uses on V.
- **Distinct from prior.** 143 (pre-attn, closed borderline), 157 (post-FFN, closed null), 158-GAU (block fusion, needs-run), 154-rebased-attn (record-breaking WIN on *K/V* pre-softmax rebiasing — pre-attention axis, complementary not duplicate). 163 is post-softmax on the *output* of attention, pre-W_O projection — third axis.
- **Portfolio OK.** Recent cluster (152-attn-logit-bias null, 153-relu2-ffn null, 154-rebased-attn WIN, 155-per-head-temp null, 156-moa null, 157-conv-ffn null) is per-head/per-block/per-axis shape work; 163 is a token-mixing prior, mechanically orthogonal. Not crowding.
- **Caveats the definition gate should weigh.** (a) At d_model=64 / 12L the channel count for the depthwise conv is 12×64=768 weights (k=3 each); the small-channel regime is exactly where Hyena's bigger kernels dominate, so k=3 is the right A/B for tiny1m3m and should be the only kernel tested. (b) Padding=1 symmetric (causal+future) is correct for a non-causal pretraining objective; do not switch to causal padding mid-run. (c) Step-0 bit-identity claim is load-bearing — definition gate must verify `fp32 max-abs-diff < 1e-6` across all 12 blocks at the no-flag path vs the flag-on path *with* the conv weight initialized post-construction via direct `.data` assignment (no RNG advance).
- **Accept and route to definition gate.** `round` resets to 1 for the definition loop.
