## r1 — 2026-06-10 — verdict: accept
- **Mechanism**: surgical. A single `nn.LayerNorm(d_head)` applied to V along the `d_head` axis after `v_proj(x).view(B,H,T,d_head)` and *before* the AV product, separate from the existing `q_norm`/`k_norm` from 016. Because each V vector is unit-RMS along `d_head`, the per-head contribution to the attention output is bounded in magnitude — outlier value-vector entries can no longer dominate the AV aggregation. ~15 LoC, mirrors the existing QK-Norm code path. Init γ=1, β=0 → step-0 is bit-identical to baseline.
- **Transferability**: the lever is *per-head output-value bounding* — strictly local to the MHA block, scale-invariant in mechanism. The closed norm-zoo (pnorm/manhattan/center/squash/clip/channelscale) all worked the *residual stream* and showed NULL. 016 (per-head Q,K) won at tiny1m3m by Δ -0.0138/-0.0185. 029 is a third site (per-head V) — neither a residual-stream repeat nor a QK repeat. The transfer-risk tag (`med`) is honest: only the Wortsman 2023 ViT/BERT diagnostic carries the mechanism at scale, and the LM-pretraining record is thin compared to QK-Norm's Qwen3/SmolLM3 adoption. But the transfer argument is anchored to 016's already-validated per-head-site, so the path to the 135M recipe is "composes with 016" rather than "needs a new adoption story."
- **Bet** (crisp): "we expect a small win (Δ -0.005 to -0.015) because the same per-head magnitude drift that helped Q,K in 016 also acts on V — outlier V entries destabilize the AV output and the residual stream, and bounding V at the head-dim level is the symmetric fix." A null partitions the cause of 016's win between logit-bounding (Q,K side) and output-value-bounding (V side) — informative regardless of outcome.
- **Null teaching**: a clean null at tiny1m3m answers a *specific* question 016's win alone could not: "was the 016 win about bounding the dot-product magnitude, or about bounding *either* projection magnitude in MHA?" That partitions the per-head-site family cleanly. The closed norm-zoo nulls couldn't partition this from a residual-stream question, and 016 alone can't partition QK-side from V-side.
