The closest in-repo nulls (152, 155, 166) are all *content-free* per-head scalars; 175 has *position-distance structure* that gives the lever a non-trivial axis the optimizer can exploit that those do not have. So this is *not* "the 4th per-head scalar lever" — the structured bias is a qualitatively different mechanism.

It's not a 5th derivative variant — it's the structurally-distinct member of the family. Locality-prior PEs (009, 174-xpos-decay) and attention shape (152, 155, 160, 166) are different niches; 175 sits in locality-prior-attention, where wins are still being banked.
