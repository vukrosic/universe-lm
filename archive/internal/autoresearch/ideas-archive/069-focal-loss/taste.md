## r1 — 2026-06-11 — verdict: revise

- **Loss-wrapper crowding (portfolio).** Active queue already holds four loss-shape bets: 010-polyloss (done, NULL: trt 6.5938 vs ctrls 6.5991/6.6050, inside variance), 066-label-smoothing (needs-taste), 067-confidence-penalty (needs-taste), 068-unlikelihood (needs-taste). To survive this, the re-pitch must say *why focal differs in kind* from a Taylor-expansion wrapper, not just in functional form.
- **Mechanism leverage is backwards at tiny1m3m.** Focal loss assumes a regime with *easy examples wasting gradient*. At 0.94M params · 3M tokens we are severely **underfit** — train loss is still well above 6, so almost every token is "hard" (p_t small). When p_t is small, (1-p_t)^γ ≈ 1 and focal loss collapses to CE. The lever has no surface to act on at this scale. The miner's "if tiny1m3m is dominated by easy-token gradients" premise is the inverse of the actual underfit regime. Re-pitch must either name the easy-token population (which token classes? frequency-binned evidence) or admit the regime mismatch.
- **Wrong objective.** Mukhoti 2020 sells focal loss as a **calibration** improvement (ECE), not a CE/perplexity improvement; their accuracy claim is "competitive", not better. The screen optimizes val CE. A lever whose *paper-stated* win is on a metric the screen doesn't measure is a poor fit — even a clean win on calibration here wouldn't earn a 135M slot because beat-smollm2-135m optimizes loss/downstream, not ECE. Re-pitch must either redirect the metric (justify calibration as a 135M-relevant proxy) or argue a CE-specific mechanism the paper doesn't.
- **Information-value framing is missing.** A defensible re-pitch route exists: "focal WILL hurt val loss at tiny1m3m because no easy tokens exist; the magnitude tells us how far from saturation we are, calibrating when future loss-wrapper screens *could* pay." That's an informative-null bet. The current pitch frames it as a winner, which polyloss already refuted in spirit. Pick a framing where both outcomes teach.
- **How to close the gap (concrete).** Either (a) drop γ as global constant and propose **token-frequency-conditioned focal** (γ scales with log-frequency rank — a real LM-specific mechanism not in Mukhoti), or (b) reframe as informative-null in §"Why it's worth a slot" with the specific p_t-distribution prediction the run would confirm, or (c) withdraw in favor of a non-loss-wrapper family — the 4 already-queued loss bets are enough portfolio coverage for this gate.
