# Review log — 202 grouped-value-projection

## r1 — 2026-06-15 — verdict: approve
- **Source check ✓.** GQA (Ainslie et al. 2023, arXiv:2305.13245) and MQA (Shazeer 2019, arXiv:1911.02150) are real, current, and 178-mqa-gated is the closed probe the idea explicitly anchors against. The `closed.md:23` arch-sweep "MHA vs GQA, MLA, Tied QK" line is also cited correctly. Nothing fabricated.
- **Mechanism is a mechanism, not an HP.** V-only soft-blend via per-head `sigmoid(α_h)` is a structural change to the V path with K held out as the control — explicitly NOT a learning-rate / init-constant lever. α init is the standard "init to off, let the optimizer learn" pattern (cf. closed 178's `mqa_gate_k/v` zero-init).
- **tiny1m3m-only ✓.** Plan specs a single `Tiny1M3MGroupedVConfig` subclass. No screen20m / full ladder / multi-tier reference. One seed (42) per prime directive.
- **Not a closed-duplicate ✓.** The closed arch-sweep is fixed-group-size GQA (K and V together); 178 is symmetric K+V gated-blend. 202 is the asymmetric V-only form — distinct lever. The idea explicitly engages with both priors and reads 178's evidence.md (the val-only recording gap is named in the source line).
- **Implementable in < 200 LoC ✓.** Sketched against the real files: MHA.__init__ adds one flag + one `nn.ParameterList` (G Linear layers) + one `nn.Parameter((H,), -10)`; forward adds one extra per-block matmul + sigmoid + per-head blend before the existing reshape. New config subclass. llm.py threads 2 flags into 2 sites. ~50-80 LoC, well under the budget. Init uses in-group mean of W_V_h (deterministic, no extra RNG).
- **Param overhead ✓.** −2.6% (replaces 49,152 per-head V projs with 24,576 group V projs + 48 α scalars → net −24,528 of 0.94M). Single extra per-block matmul → +5% FLOPs ish, within the noise band.
- **Falsifiable bar ✓.** Primary metric is σ(α_h) trajectory, secondary is val Δ. Box noise ±0.01 is bypassed by the per-head gate read-out (the σ trajectory is not val-loss-noise-bounded; it either moves or it doesn't). Four distinct outcomes (a-d) with distinct downstream consequences; the val-Δ null from 178 is acknowledged as not-enough-to-close-mechanistically and 202 produces the attribution.
- **transfer-risk: med ✓.** Tag is correctly justified in the `## Scale evidence` section — the family is well-validated at Llama-2 7B/13B/70B but the asymmetric V-only form is novel with no published scale. The justification is in the idea, not asserted.
- **Findings to fix in plan.md (implementer attention, not blocking).**
  1. **Step-0 byte-identity is approximate, not bit-identical.** σ(α=-10) ≈ 4.5e-5, not 0. At fp32 this is `4.5e-5 · (V_group − V_h_local)`, which is non-zero. The smoke test's max-abs-diff < 0 will fail. The implementer should set `v_group_alpha_init = -25.0` (σ ≈ 1.4e-11, well below fp32 precision) or document that the lever ships with a ~4.5e-5 step-0 deviation. The structural intent ("start at no-sharing, let α learn") is sound either way.
  2. **α dimension must be H, not n_kv_heads.** The idea uses H=4 (per-head, not per-KV-head) — that's correct for the V-blend (each head picks its own share of the group V). 178 used `n_kv_heads` because the gated-blend was per-KV-head; 202's is per-head by design. Implementer should NOT reuse 178's `mqa_gate_v.shape = n_kv_heads` pattern.
  3. **W_V_group init must be a true mean, not the per-head W_V_h reshaped.** `W_V_group_g = mean(W_V_h for h in group g)`. Do this once at construction; don't add a per-step EMA (it would shift step-0 and complicate the smoke).
  4. **Trajectory dump — explicit schema.** Plan must specify: dump at end of run a single tensor `v_group_alpha_final` of shape `[n_layers, H]` (12 × 4 = 48 scalars) plus a `v_group_alpha_min/max/mean` summary over the 92-step run. Cost negligible; no second run needed.
- **Why this is approved, not revised.** The mechanism is sound, the bet is sharp, the lever is distinct from the two closed priors, the LoC is realistic, the metrics are recorded, and the four outcomes are all informative. The bit-identity concern (finding 1) is a one-line init tweak at implementation time, not a definition-gate block. Round reset to 1 for the code gate.
