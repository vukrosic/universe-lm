# Vision

1. **Build open tooling for LLM pretraining research** — make it faster and cheaper for anyone to train LLMs, by discovering optimizations in the open and shipping them as reusable recipes, configs, and code.

2. **Prove the tooling works by releasing competitive models** — measure against other models (e.g. on artificialanalysis.ai).

- Today we compete on efficiency (intelligence-per-dollar); when compute partners land, we scale the same recipes toward absolute intelligence — MMLU-Pro, GPQA, math, coding - competiting with all other models - this will ensure that your open research direction is helpful to everyone else.
